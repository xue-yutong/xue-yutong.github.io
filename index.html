About

I am a medical student interested in applying quantitative and computational methods to clinically meaningful questions. My current work includes early outcome prediction after kidney transplantation and the study of host factors involved in human adenovirus infection.

Current Research

Kidney Transplantation and Early Outcome Prediction

Studying whether early post-transplant creatinine measurements can predict medium- and long-term graft function, with an emphasis on transparent model evaluation and clinically interpretable results.

Host Factors in Human Adenovirus Infection

Investigating host genes that influence viral replication through computational screening and experimental validation, including knockdown and CRISPR-based approaches.

AI-assisted Bowel Sound Detection

Developing methods for automated identification and analysis of bowel sounds from physiological audio recordings as part of a research project currently being prepared for submission.
